ABOUT ME

If you`re wondering who I am...

I`m Marco, a 26 years old self-taught Front-end developer, from Italy

After finishing my diploma in IT (Perito Informatio), I decided to relocate to London, UK, there I stayed for almost 6 years. I worked a variety of jobs, spanning from catering, construction to transporation. During those years I learned many important lessons and skills that, I found, transfer directly and indirectly to my Developer career. However after a few years I felt like I wasn`t learning and improving, I felt stuck.

In March 2018, after a minor car accident while I was working my courier job, I decided it was time for a change, it was time to go back to my original passion, programming! I started waking up at 6am to study HTML, CSS and Javascript before going to work. I quickly started to love Javascript, gradually I dedicated more and more time to studying Web Development and in January 2019 I decided to take the risk and quite my job. I`ve been studying and freelancing Front-end development full time ever since.

During this time, I won a scholarship to study Front-end Web Development with OpenClassrooms, an accredited French education provider. I also took a number of online courses like: Modern Javascript from the beginning Bootsrap 4 From Scratch Advanced Javascript Concepts, many more HTML & CSS courses from TeamTreehouse, read the book series You Don`t Know JS and watched countless youtube videos about Web Development.

Because we know the best way to learn is to practice, I also did a bunch of personal projects with the knowledge gathered from the courses, you can check them on my portfolio.

I always want to learn more, do more and be more. I`m also a firm believer that we should always strive to do better.

I`m hard working, super curious, passionate, commited, and also a fast learner!

My current stack of languages/technologies is:

HTML5 - CSS3 - JAVASCRIPT - SASS - WORDPRESS - GIT - BOOTSTRAP - TAILWINDCSS
